Shahrisabz

Shahrisabz is one of the oldest cities in Central Asia. In the past, the city was an important urban center in Sogdiana, a province of the Achaemenid Empire in Persia.

Aydar Lake, Gurun Kyzylkum

is an artificial reservoir created in the 1960s, at the same time as the construction of the Aydar-Arnasay Dam on the Syr Darya River. Located in the Kyzylkum Desert, Lake Aydar is a refreshing oasis in an arid region.

Charvak Reservoir, Tashkent

Located at the foot of the Chatkal Mountains, Charvak Reservoir is an artificial lake created by forming a dam on the Chirciq River. This lake offers beautiful views of a turquoise blue lake surrounded by lush green mountains.
